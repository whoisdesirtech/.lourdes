## 3. Typography System

### Headings / Wordmark: Spencerian Script `[CONFIRMED]` `[HIGH CONFIDENCE]`
* **Style characteristics**: Elegant, flowing 19th-century Spencerian calligraphy, featuring dramatic loops, curved tails, and script ligatures.
* **Google/Adobe Fonts Match**: *Great Vibes* (Google Fonts) or *Alex Brush* (Google Fonts)
* **Fallback Stack**: `cursive`
* **Rationale**: Used exclusively for the logo wordmark. It is a highly guarded, custom trademark font.

### Heading Typeface (Marketing): TCCC Unity (Bold/Regular) `[CONFIRMED]` `[HIGH CONFIDENCE]`
* **Style characteristics**: Custom geometric sans-serif designed for Coca-Cola. It features clean circular forms, a modern x-height, and highly readable terminal cuts.
* **Google/Adobe Fonts Match**: *Montserrat* or *Outfit*
* **Fallback Stack**: `Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif`
* **Rationale**: The official corporate and marketing font family created by Brody Associates for digital and print typography.

### Body Text Typeface: TCCC Unity Text `[CONFIRMED]` `[HIGH CONFIDENCE]`
* **Style characteristics**: A slightly wider, highly legible sans-serif variant of TCCC Unity optimized for body layouts.
* **Google/Adobe Fonts Match**: *Inter* or *Roboto*
* **Fallback Stack**: `sans-serif`
* **Rationale**: Used for large text blocks, fine print, nutrition facts, and legal disclaimers.

### Font Pairings Rationale
The pairing of the flowing, historic Spencerian script (wordmark) with the clean, structured, geometric sans-serif (TCCC Unity) creates a perfect tension between legacy heritage and modern simplicity, conveying a friendly, bold, and universal aesthetic.

## 4. Logo Usage Guidelines

### Observed Variations
1. **Classic Red-on-White**: Red script wordmark on a white background (often used in light mode applications).
2. **Classic White-on-Red**: White script wordmark on a solid Coca-Cola Red background (standard packaging).
3. **The Spencerian Script Wordmark with Dynamic Ribbon / Wave**: The wordmark accompanied by the dual white/silver wave sweeping underneath.

### Clear Space Rules
* Maintain a minimum clear space of `0.5x` (where `x` is the height of the capital "C" in the wordmark) around the entire logo structure. No text or overlapping imagery should enter this margin.

### Sizing Rules
* **Minimum print size**: 0.75 inches (19mm) wide.
* **Minimum digital size**: 80px wide.
* **Aspect ratio**: Always maintain the exact horizontal script proportions (do not compress or stretch).

### Do's and Don'ts
* **DO**: Place the logo horizontally.
* **DO**: Keep high contrast (white on Coca-Cola Red is preferred).
* **DON'T**: Re-color the script logo in unauthorized colors (e.g., blue, green).
* **DON'T**: Distort, stretch, or rotate the wordmark.
* **DON'T**: Apply gradients or drop shadows to the wordmark in clean modern layouts.
